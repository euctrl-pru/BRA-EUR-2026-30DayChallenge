## COMAC C909 / C919 public facts dataset

File: `data/comac-c909-c919-public-facts-2024-2026.csv`

This small reference dataset was assembled manually on April 24, 2026 from public webpages rather than downloaded from a single publisher-provided machine-readable source.

It combines:

* official COMAC product pages for aircraft specifications and milestone dates
* official COMAC news releases for deliveries, passenger milestones, and export milestones
* one South China Morning Post article for the latest reported 2026 C919 delivery context

The CSV keeps one fact per row and includes:

* `source_org`
* `source_title`
* `source_date`
* `source_url`
* `assembly_note`

Suggested caption note:

"Data assembled manually from public COMAC product/news pages and SCMP reporting on April 24, 2026; one row per published fact, with source URLs included in the CSV for reproducibility."
