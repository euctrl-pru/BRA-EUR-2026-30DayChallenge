### Option 1

Day 24 of the #30DayChartChallenge: `South China Morning Post`

No ready-made dataset this time, so we assembled a small public-facts file from COMAC product pages, COMAC news releases, and recent SCMP reporting.

Today’s chart compares COMAC’s `C909` and `C919` across:

* design envelope
* current public operational scale
* the recent SCMP signal on the slower C919 delivery pace in Q1 2026

The two aircraft do not target the same market segment, but the public counts still reveal a clear gap in operating exposure and ramp-up stage.

Data file used for the chart is available in the repo for reproducibility:
`data/comac-c909-c919-public-facts-2024-2026.csv`
